## Notes and Caveats

1. **Director Loan vs. Salary vs. Dividends:** The statement distinguishes between Director Loan advances (labelled "Director Loan [Name]"), payroll runs (labelled "From ACUE LTD"), dividends (labelled "[Initials] Dividends" or "[Initials] Dividend"), and expenses. All are included in the OUT totals above.

2. **Repayments:** Carl made partial repayments in three periods: a creditnote and DL payback totalling ~£38,270 in Year 1 (including a £34,000 direct payback and a £4,200 creditnote), a £55,006 DL repayment in November 2022 (Year 2), and £5,466 in June 2024 (Year 4). A £9.99 October 2023 transfer appears to be an accidental return. Catherine made repayments of £34,000 in December 2021 and £50,000 in November 2022. These are all reflected as IN from each individual and reduce their net figures.
